Arlene Carr

Contemporary Abstract Artist

Arlene Carr is a contemporary abstract artist whose work explores the intersection of emotion, color, and form. Based in the heart of the creative district, her paintings are characterized by bold brushstrokes, vibrant palettes, and an intuitive approach to composition that invites viewers into a dialogue with the canvas.

With over two decades of artistic practice, Carr's work has been exhibited in galleries and private collections internationally. Her artistic journey began with formal training in fine arts, but it was her personal exploration of abstract expressionism that truly defined her unique voice. Each piece she creates is a meditation on the human experience—capturing moments of joy, struggle, transformation, and transcendence.

Carr's process is both disciplined and spontaneous. She works primarily with acrylics and mixed media, layering textures and colors to build depth and complexity. Her studio practice involves extended periods of experimentation, allowing the work to evolve organically. The result is a body of work that feels alive, dynamic, and deeply personal while remaining universally accessible.
